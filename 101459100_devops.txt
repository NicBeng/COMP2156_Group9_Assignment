In this class, students will get to know how a DevOps engineer helps make a workplace more efficient. They will:

- Look into different ways DevOps is used and get hands-on experience with common tools used in the industry.
- Learn how to solve complex problems in business settings by following what DevOps engineers do.
- Study different data formats like XML, JSON, YAML, and YANG. These are important for making networks work better automatically.
- Get to use important DevOps tools, such as Ansible and Git, for real tasks.
